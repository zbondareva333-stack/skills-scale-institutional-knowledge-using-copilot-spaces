# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered experiences by designing interfaces, conducting usability reviews, and ensuring accessibility standards are met. They collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers to translate requirements into intuitive, accessible designs.

### Responsibilities
- Design user experiences and interfaces that meet usability standards
- Conduct usability reviews and accessibility audits
- Ensure WCAG compliance and inclusive design practices
- Collaborate on product specifications and design system maintenance
- Provide design feedback during code reviews and QA processes
- Document design decisions and create design specifications

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction
- Maintain design consistency across the product
- Enable developers to implement designs accurately

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- Accessibility audit reports
- Design specifications and component documentation
- User research findings and usability testing results

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define, track, and report key metrics that inform product prioritization and business decisions. They partner with Product Managers to establish success criteria and collaborate with engineering on instrumentation and data quality.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track product metrics aligned with business goals
- Analyze product usage patterns and user behavior
- Conduct retrospective analysis and trend identification
- Partner with Product Managers on goal setting and prioritization
- Collaborate with Development and QA on instrumentation and data collection
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholder visibility

### Goals
- Provide data-driven insights for prioritization
- Ensure metrics align with product and business objectives
- Enable evidence-based decision making
- Maintain data quality and integrity

### Typical Communication
- Metric dashboards and regular analytics reports
- Goal-setting and retrospective analysis sessions
- Data quality and instrumentation design reviews
- Insight presentations to stakeholders and leadership

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and orchestrate all release activities, including staging and production deployments. They maintain release schedules, manage rollback protocols, and ensure coordination across teams during release windows.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate and schedule releases according to business requirements
- Orchestrate staging and production deployments
- Maintain and publish release calendars
- Develop and execute rollback protocols and contingency plans
- Coordinate with DevOps, QA, and Project Managers on release readiness
- Document release notes and communicate release status to stakeholders
- Track and resolve release-related issues and blockers

### Goals
- Execute releases with minimal risk and disruption
- Maintain clear communication and transparency during releases
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) through effective rollback processes
- Improve release velocity and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Release schedules and deployment timelines
- Release readiness checklists and sign-offs
- Release notes and deployment documentation
- Post-release retrospectives and incident reports

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure that security best practices are integrated throughout the product lifecycle. They oversee threat modeling, review production deployments for security concerns, and lead security incident response efforts.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct and oversee threat modeling for new features
- Review and approve security aspects of production deployments
- Lead security incident identification, response, and remediation
- Establish and maintain security standards and guidelines
- Conduct security code reviews and architecture reviews
- Collaborate with teams on security requirements and compliance

### Goals
- Integrate security throughout the product development lifecycle
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and incidents
- Maintain compliance with security standards and regulations
- Enable secure product delivery without sacrificing velocity

### Typical Communication
- Threat modeling and security review sessions
- Security incident reports and post-incident reviews
- Security architecture and design guidance
- Compliance and standards documentation

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads serve as the voice of the customer, collecting feedback, triaging issues, and ensuring customer readiness for releases. They bridge the gap between customers and the product team to improve user experience and support quality.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and communicate customer feedback to Product and Development teams
- Triage and prioritize user-reported bugs and feature requests
- Update and maintain customer-facing documentation
- Surface usage trends and customer pain points to inform prioritization
- Ensure support readiness and training before product releases
- Coordinate with Product and Development teams on product issues
- Participate in release planning and post-release support

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume through better documentation and design
- Enable faster resolution of customer issues
- Provide actionable feedback to the product team

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
- Bug reports and feature request prioritization
- Support readiness checklists and training materials
- Customer-facing documentation and release notes
